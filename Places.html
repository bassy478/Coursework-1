Places I've Visited.

Dorset


I visited Dorset for a week which is a county in South West England on the English Channel coast.
We stayed in a cottage and explored the country side whilst stopping at some of my Favourite second hand book stores.
We stayed here during covid which meant we couldn't visit any coffee shops however, the fresh air and stunning views were more than satisfactory, a week was not enough to explore the beauty of Dorset.


My favourite attractions were:


  1. Corfe Castle
  2. Durdle Door
  3. Bridport Old Books







Cambridge


Cambdridge is a city on the River Cam in eastern England, home to the prestigious University of Cambridge
I got to visit some of the most famous cambridge attractions and a large range of museums
Despite the weather being gloomy most of the time, it did not change the beauty of the scenery and overall experience. There were also plenty of coffee shops to hide in :)


My favourite attractions were:


  1. The Centre for Computing History
  2. Cambridge Central Mosque
  3. Heffers Bookshop












Morocco


This is a country in the north west of Africa. I visit Morocco every year to see my family but am always excited and amazed by getting to explore the different cities and gain a deeper appreciation for my culture. People usually visit Marrakesh and Casablanca, but theres something different about getting to visit areas of the country that grasp onto the depths of the culture.

My favourite attractions were:


  1. Loudaya
  2. Bab el Had Square
  3. Hassan Tower
